ABOUT US

This school was founded in 1990.With continuous support and cooperation of very Rev.Fr.M.M.Sammanasu, the then Parish Priest of Villankanni, this school was elevated to the status of Matriculation School and later on the sisters of IHM, Pondichery handed over the school administration to the diocese on 6th of May,2010. Now the School is run by the diocese of Thanjavur Society and managed by Most Rev.M.Devadass Ambrose D.D.,L.S.S.,S.T.D., the Bishop of Thanjavur.The prime motive of our school is providing
value based quality and holistic education to one and all, irrespective of caste,creed ,religion etc.
